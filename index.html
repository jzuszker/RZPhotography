ABOUT

The story behind the lens and the philosophy that drives my work.

Hello, I'm Alex

I'm a visual storyteller with a passion for capturing the extraordinary in ordinary moments. My journey in photography began over a decade ago when I picked up my first film camera, and since then, I've been exploring the world through my lens.

My work focuses on minimalism, emotion, and the interplay of light and shadow. I believe that photography is not just about capturing what we see, but about conveying how we feel in that moment.

Based in New York, I travel frequently to document diverse cultures, landscapes, and urban environments. My work has been featured in several publications and exhibitions worldwide.
